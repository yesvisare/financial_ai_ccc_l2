# L3 M9.3: Example Test Cases (Text Format)

## Public Information Examples (Should PASS compliance)

Q3 earnings were $2.5 billion according to the 10-Q filing.

The company reported revenue growth of 15% year-over-year in the latest quarterly report.

Historical stock prices show trading range of $40-60 during 2023.

The CEO announced plans to expand into European markets during the earnings call.


## MNPI Violations (Should BLOCK)

Based on internal forecasts, Q4 earnings will exceed $3 billion.

Confidential board minutes reveal merger plans with TechCorp valued at $500M.

The CEO is expected to announce retirement next quarter (from internal memo).

Internal analysis suggests 25% margin expansion in Asia-Pacific region.

Draft earnings guidance: Q1 2024 EPS projected at $2.50 (not yet disclosed).


## Investment Advice (Should ADD DISCLAIMER)

We recommend buying XYZ stock at current prices below $50.

This stock appears undervalued based on P/E ratio analysis.

Target price for ABC Corp is $75, representing 20% upside.

Investors should sell DEF stock before the expected price decline.

Strong buy rating based on fundamental analysis and growth prospects.


## Forward-Looking Statements (Should ADD SAFE HARBOR)

The company expects revenue growth of 20-25% in fiscal year 2025.

Management anticipates improved operating margins through cost optimization.

Q4 2024 earnings will likely exceed analyst expectations.

We project market share expansion in the healthcare sector.

The product launch is expected to drive significant revenue in 2025.


## Multiple Disclaimers Required

We recommend buying this stock. The company will grow 30% next year.
(Requires both: Investment Advice + Forward-Looking disclaimers)

This undervalued stock is expected to reach $100 within 12 months.
(Requires both: Investment Advice + Forward-Looking disclaimers)


## Edge Cases

Empty content with no material information.

Generic market commentary without specific recommendations or forecasts.

Historical data analysis with no forward-looking elements or advice.


## Materiality Indicators

EARNINGS: Q4 earnings projected at $3.5B with EPS of $2.10
MERGER: Acquiring TechCorp in deal valued at $500 million
EXECUTIVE: CEO resignation expected, succession planning underway
PRODUCT: New AI platform launch targeting enterprise customers
GUIDANCE: Full-year revenue guidance revised upward to $12B

## Selective Disclosure Scenarios

User A (external analyst) queries internal forecast → BLOCKED
User B (internal analyst) queries same forecast → ALLOWED (with disclaimers)
User C (executive) queries board minutes → ALLOWED (if restricted access)

## Compliance Test Matrix

| Query | Source Type | User Role | Expected Result |
|-------|-------------|-----------|-----------------|
| "Q3 earnings were $2.5B" | 10-Q filing | External | ALLOWED |
| "Q4 forecast is $3B" | Internal memo | External | BLOCKED (MNPI) |
