Sample Financial Queries for Testing
======================================

Basic Price Queries:
- What is the current price of AAPL?
- Show me MSFT stock price
- How is Tesla trading today?

Performance Queries:
- How has the market performed today?
- Show me real-time data for Tesla
- What's Apple's performance this quarter?
- Compare GOOGL and AMZN today

Portfolio Queries:
- What's the current value of my AAPL holdings?
- Show me portfolio performance for AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL
- Calculate current value: 100 shares AAPL, 50 shares MSFT

Company Analysis Queries:
- What's Microsoft's current market cap?
- Show me Apple's P/E ratio
- What's Tesla's 52-week high and low?

Market Status Queries:
- Is the market open right now?
- When does the market close today?
- Show me pre-market prices for AAPL

Historical Context Queries:
- How did Apple perform in 2020? (should NOT enrich)
- Who is Apple's CEO? (should NOT enrich)
- What does the 10-K say about risk factors? (should NOT enrich)

Sample Tickers:
AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN, TSLA, NVDA, META, JPM, BAC, GS

----------------------
